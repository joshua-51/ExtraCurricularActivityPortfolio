This is the basic loop:

i = 3
while i != 0:
    print("meow")

This while continue printing meow forever

i = 3
while i != 0:
    print("meow")
    i = i - 1

This will run three times and then stop

for loops:

for i in [0, 1, 2]:
    print("meow")

Basically the same thing

for i in range(3):
    print("meow")

When you do not care about the variable:

for _ in range(3):
    print("meow")

You can also write This

print("meow\n" * 3, end="")
